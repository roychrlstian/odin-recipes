Burger Recipe

A classic beef burger is a simple yet satisfying meal made with a juicy beef patty, fresh vegetables, and a toasted bun. It's perfect for casual dinners or outdoor gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 4 burger buns
  • 4 slices of cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion slices, pickles (for toppings)
  • Ketchup, mustard, mayo (for condiments)

Steps

  1. In a bowl, combine ground beef, chopped onion, minced garlic, salt, and pepper. Mix until just combined, being careful not to overwork the meat.
  2. Form the meat mixture into 4 patties, being careful not to overwork the meat.
  3. Preheat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Grill or cook the patties for 3â€“4 minutes per side, depending on desired doneness.
  5. Place cheese slices on top of patties during the last minute of cooking if using.
  6. Toast the burger buns lightly on the grill or in a toaster.
  7. Assemble burgers by placing patties on bottom buns, adding toppings and condiments as desired.
